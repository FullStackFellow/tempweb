Edible Inks

Both our edible natural inks, and our edible artificial inks, are filtered to 0.5 microns. They do not clog inkjet printer nozzles. Of course, edible artificial color inks mirror the inks in your computer’s desktop printer. Edible natural inks are close in hue but are not an exact match. Please mix free samples of our edible natural inks and see how colorful CYMK may be.
