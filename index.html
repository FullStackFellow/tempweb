Food And Beverage

ColorMaker has developed custom natural color blends since 1996. Our color blends are compatible with our customer’s product, process, and packaging requirements. We understand the differences between RTD beverages and powdered beverages, between tetra-pak soups and 5-log reduction juices, and between high-fat ice cream and non-dairy cheese! Let us improve the visual appeal of your company’s food or beverage product.

Personal Care Products

Personal care products are diverse, from liquid soaps and moisturizers to shampoos and sunscreens, or from body scrubs to self-tanners. Our natural color blends are water-soluble dyes; they are not insoluble pigments. Therefore, our color blends do not function in cosmetic products (such as eye liner, mascara, foundation, lipstick, or blush). We help personal care products bloom with natural color.

Pet Food And Treats

From dog treats to refrigerated plant-based protein meals, dogs (and dog owners) have more choices than ever before. Gone are the days of one-size-fits-all extruded dry pet food. Gone also are the days of crushing cost constraints. Natural colors are found in bird feed, fish food, and dog treats. Let us improve the visual appeal of your company’s pet food or treat.

Edible Inks

Printing inks come in four colors: CYMK – cyan, yellow, magenta, and black. By mixing these four colors, inkjet printers create millions of different shades and reproduce detailed four-color images onto the surfaces of cakes, cookies, and other products. Whether your company is marking eggs or printing four-color pictures onto the surface of birthday cakes, we deliver edible inks to dazzle your customers.
